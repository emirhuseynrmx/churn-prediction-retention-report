# Client Intake Form

Use this before starting a paid churn report.

## Dataset

- What file will you provide? CSV or Excel?
- What does one row represent?
- What is the customer ID column?
- What is the churn target column?
- What values mean churned and retained?
- What time period does the dataset cover?
- Are there duplicate customers?
- Are there columns that should not be used for modeling?

## Churn Definition

- What counts as churn for your business?
- Is churn voluntary cancellation, non-payment, inactivity, downgrade, or something else?
- What date range should define the churn label?
- Should recently acquired customers be excluded?

## Retention Actions

- Which retention channels are available: email, call, SMS, in-app message, discount, onboarding?
- Which actions are not allowed?
- Should high-risk customers be handled by sales, support, or customer success?
- How many customers can the team realistically contact per week?

## Privacy

- Can sensitive columns be removed or anonymized before delivery?
- Should the raw dataset be deleted after project completion?
- Who is allowed to receive the final PDF and CSV outputs?

## Reporting

- Who is the report for: founder, sales, customer success, analytics, or investors?
- Do you need technical details, business summary, or both?
- Should the report use your company language or terminology?
